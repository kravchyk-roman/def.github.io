FAQs

I sold an NFT, wheres my SOL?

Trades are settled in WSOL, which is a wrapped version of SOL used on SOL Smart Chain. That means that when you sell an item, WSOL is sent to your wallet instead of SOL.

You can instantly swap your WSOL for SOL with no trading fees on Cleandefi.

When can I trade other NFT Collections?

In the new IFO 3.1 format. There is a brand new Private Sale. To participate, participants will have to meet certain requirements presented on the IFO card. Each eligible participant will be able to commit any amount of BNB up to the maximum commit limit, which is published along with the IFO voting proposal. The Private Sale has no participation fee.

In the Public Sale, everyone with an active Cleandefi Swap profile can commit. However the maximum amount of BNB user can commit, is equal to the average BNB balance in the IFO BNB pool prior to the IFO. And theres a fee for participation: see below.
